Intro

As a child, I loved storytelling, and now as an adult, I fuel that passion into data storytelling. Every data has a narrative, and I love uncovering them. As a Data Scientist and enthusiast, new things intrigue me, creating a zeal for continuous learning. I thrive on using data to draw insights and help companies make informed decisions to drive business growth and mitigate costs.

With a Master's degree in Business Analytics & Graduate certificate in Applied Machine Learning & Generative AI from The University of Texas at Dallas and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Malla Reddy Engineering College for Women - Hyderabad, I leverage my unique designing perspective with analytical skills to deliver Out of the Box results.

During my tenure as a Data Scientist at Varsun eTechnologies, I developed and optimized SQL scripts for Big Data on the BigQuery database, executed Agile SDLC to optimize marketing strategies, and pioneered cross-functional team collaborations to build data pipelines, models, and dashboards. My work consistently delivered significant improvements in data integrity, operational efficiency, and user conversion rates.
